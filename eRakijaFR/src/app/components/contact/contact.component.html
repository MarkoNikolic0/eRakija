TASTE OUR RAKIJA

Rakija is a popular fruit brandy in the Balkans, similar to brandy or vodka. It's typically around 40% alcohol by volume (ABV), but homemade rakija can be much stronger. You'll find it spelled and pronounced in a few different ways depending on the country: rakia, rakija, rachiu, or raki.
